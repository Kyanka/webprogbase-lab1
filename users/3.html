BIO

Vagn Fragnko (August 27, 1856, Naguyevychi, May 28, 1916, Lviv, Austria-Hungary) is a prominent Ukrainian writer, poet, publicist, translator, scholar, public and political figure. Doctor of Philosophy (1893), Full Member of Shevchenko Scientific Society (1899), Honorary Doctor of Kharkiv University (1906)
